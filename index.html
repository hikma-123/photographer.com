About Malcolm

Based on the wild and beautiful North West coast of Scotland, I've been capturing the stunning landscapes and wildlife of this region for over 15 years. My photography is driven by a deep connection to the natural world and a desire to share its wonder with others.

What began as a passion for documenting Scotland's dramatic scenery has evolved into a full-time career encompassing not just nature photography, but also weddings, portraits, and special events - all approached with the same eye for natural beauty and authentic moments.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me hiking the coastal paths, kayaking the lochs, or simply watching the ever-changing light dance across the landscape - often with my camera close at hand, just in case.
